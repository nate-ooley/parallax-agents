How it works

From idea to autonomous workforce
in three moves.

01

Design

Describe the role in plain language or start from a template. Parallax assembles the agent — model, tools, memory, and permissions — and previews it instantly.

  • Visual + prompt-driven builder
  • Bring your own tools & data
  • Guardrails and approvals built in
02

Deploy

Launch one agent or a swarm of thirty across Anthropic, OpenAI, and Gemini accounts. Parallax handles provisioning, routing, and orchestration.

  • 20–30 agents per swarm
  • Multi-provider, per-agent accounts
  • Coordinated hand-offs & escalation
03

Scale

Watch the swarm work in real time. Track tasks, cost, and outcomes, then clone what works and assign a swarm to every employee on the team.

  • Live throughput & cost analytics
  • One-click clone & assign
  • Enterprise controls & audit logs
